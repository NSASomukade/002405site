// Blog Info

Analysis Blogs are, as the title states, for analysis. I don't claim to be any kind of professional and anything I say is based purely on my own experiences, and perhaps even just making shit up. Mostly I just try to practice my critical thinking skills on something that isn't too stressful. It may have some entertainment value! So if you're interested in poetry, watch me fumble trying to explain why I like the things I like :>

Similarly, Personal Blogs are what it says on the tin: things I'm thinking about, emotions I'm having. I can be a little melodramatic sometimes but I think it's good to get it out somewhere. Be aware that content likely will not be warned for, especially if I happen to post a vent (shouldn't be too often.)

Miscellaneous Blogs will just be things I can't fit in either of those categories. I can't tell you what to expect.
