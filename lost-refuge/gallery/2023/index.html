Lost Refuge / Gallery / 2023

2021-2022 were the years I first tried my hand at fanart--at least, fanart that wasn't OC-inserts or the like. I'd been oddly afraid to draw from the media I liked until reading The Murderbot Diaries. By 2023 I was comfortable drawing fanart so when I got into COD I was able to produce a few solid pieces (as well as some doodles that aren't posted here, sorry.) Largely, though, the personal art is lacking for this year. Most of what you're seeing are requests or commissions. I'm no less proud of them, but still, the lack of drawing for myself left me slightly wrong-footed; although, I seem to have started writing fanfiction as a sort of creative-compensation.
