Lost Refuge / Gallery / 2021 - 1

Some of the works I'm most proud of were made in 2021. I'm not usually very concerned about my artistic growth or comparing myself to other artists, but the exception appears to be comparing my current art to what I was able to do in 2021. The time I had to spend on it, as well as the patience, is something I just simply don't have today and I believe that the quality of my work has suffered because of it. I try not to let it bring me down and remember that this is in my past; if I've done it once before, the odds are good that I can do it again. also plenty of the things I made in 2021 are shit lmao.
